Normal Typing

Key Features of Normal-type:
Strengths: Normal-type Pokémon and moves do not have any type that they are super effective against. Their neutral nature makes them versatile but lacking in offensive power compared to other types.
Weaknesses: Fighting-type moves are super effective against Normal-type Pokémon, making them vulnerable to physically aggressive opponents.
Immunities: Ghost-type moves have no effect on Normal-type Pokémon. This can be a strategic advantage, as Normal-types cannot be damaged by Ghost-type moves unless the opponent uses a move like Foresight or Odor Sleuth, which nullifies this immunity.
Defensive Resistances: Normal-type Pokémon do not resist any specific type of moves other than having immunity to Ghost-type moves.

Fire Typing

Strengths: Fire-types moves are super effective against: Grass, Bug, Ice, Steel
Weakness: Fire-types are weak against: Water, Rock, Ground
Resistances: Fire-types are resistant to: Bug, Steel, Fairy, Fire
Immunities: Fire-types have no immunities

Water Typing

Strengths: Water-types moves are super effective against: Fire, Ground, Rock
Weakness: Water-types are weak against: Electric, Grass
Resistances: Water-types are resistant to: Fire, Water, Ice, Steel
Immunities: Water-types have no immunities
