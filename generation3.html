Pokemon Generation

Generation 7(2016)

Generation 7 of Pokémon introduced new gameplay styles, mechanics, and regional forms of classic Pokémon.

Basic Info:

Games: Pokémon Sun and Moon, Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon, and Let's Go, Pikachu! and Let's Go, Eevee!
Region: Alola.
Number of Pokemon: 88 new Pokémon, bringing the total to 809.
Release Year: 2016(Worldwide).

Starter Pokemon:

Alola Starters:Chooese between Rowlet (Grass/Flying), Litten (Fire), and Popplio (Water).

Key Features:

Z-Moves: Powerful one-time moves that can be used by any Pokémon holding a Z-Crystal, with special animations for each type or Pokémon.
Regional Variants: Older Pokémon got new forms unique to Alola, called Alolan Forms (e.g., Alolan Exeggutor, Alolan Vulpix), with different types and designs.
Trial Instead of Gyms: Replaced the traditional Gym system with island challenges and Totem Pokémon battles.
Ultra Beast: For the first time, you could battle other trainers online.Mysterious, powerful Pokémon from another dimension, with unique designs and lore.

Ride Pokemon: Instead of HMs, you used Ride Pokémon to traverse the world, making traveling much easier.

Pokemon Refresh: A feature to care for and bond with your Pokémon by feeding and grooming them.

Legendary Pokemon:

Main Legendaries: Solgaleo (Sun), Lunala (Moon), and Necrozma (Ultra Sun/Ultra Moon).

Other Legendaries: Zygarde’s complete form, Tapu Guardians (Tapu Koko, Tapu Lele, Tapu Bulu, Tapu Fini), and Ultra Beasts.

Villain :

Team Skull: A gang of disorganized troublemakers led by Guzma.

Aether Foundation: Initially a seemingly benevolent organization, but its president, Lusamine, had darker plans involving the Ultra Beasts.

Legacy:

Generation 7 was praised for its bold departure from the traditional formula, especially with the removal of Gyms and the addition of Regional Forms. The Hawaiian-inspired Alola region was a fresh, colorful setting, and Z-Moves added more tactical depth. It also introduced popular Pokémon like Incineroar, Mimikyu, and Lycanroc.

Generation 8(2019)

Generation 8 of Pokémon introduced new gameplay mechanics, a fresh region, and various features aimed at enhancing the overall experience.

Basic Info:

Games:Pokémon Sword and Shield (with The Isle of Armor and The Crown Tundra expansions).
Region: Galar.
Number of Pokemon: 89 new Pokémon, bringing the total to 898.
Release Year: 2019 (Worldwide).

Starter Pokemon:

Galar Starters:Chooese between Grookey (grass), Scorbunny (fire), and Sobble (water).

Key Features:

Dynamax and Gigantimax:
Dynamax allows Pokémon to grow giant in battles for three turns, boosting their power and changing their moves.
Gigantimax is a special form of Dynamaxing that gives certain Pokémon unique appearances and moves.

Wild Area: An open-world zone where players can explore, encounter Pokémon, and participate in Max Raid Battles against Dynamax Pokémon.
Pokemon Camp: Players can set up camp, cook curry, and interact with their Pokémon, fostering bonds and improving friendship.
Exp. Candy: Instead of traditional experience points, players can use candies to level up Pokémon quickly.
Regional Variants: Introduced new Galarian forms of existing Pokémon, such as Galarian Meowth and Galarian Weezing.

Legendary Pokemon:

Main Legendaries: Zacian and Zamazenta.

Other Legendaries: Eternatus, as well as new forms of existing Pokémon like the legendary birds (Galarian Articuno, Galarian Zapdos, and Galarian Moltres).

Villain:

Team Yell: A group of enthusiastic supporters for a rival trainer, Marnie, but they often cause trouble.
Rose and Macro Cosmos: Chairman Rose of the Galar region’s Pokémon League had ulterior motives tied to Eternatus and the energy crisis.

Expansion:

The Isle of Armor: A DLC focusing on training and a new area filled with wild Pokémon and new challenges.
The Crown Tundra: A DLC centered on exploration, legendary Pokémon, and a more open-ended experience.

Legacy:

Generation 8 is notable for its modern gameplay features, the introduction of Dynamaxing, and the expansive Wild Area, allowing for more exploration and interaction with Pokémon. It faced mixed reviews regarding the Pokémon available, but it still introduced many beloved characters and mechanics, such as the engaging story of Galar and the vibrant design of the region. The expansions further enriched the experience with new areas and Pokémon to discover.

Generation 9(2022)

Generation 9 of Pokémon introduced new gameplay mechanics and an expansive open-world experience.

Basic Info:

Games: Pokémon Scarlet and Pokémon Violet.
Region: Paldea.
Number of Pokemon: 103 new Pokémon, bringing the total to 1,007.
Release Year: 2022 (Worldwide).

Starter Pokemon:

Paldea Starters:Chooese between Sprigatito (grass), Fuecoco (fire), and Quaxly (water).

Key Features:

Open World: For the first time, players can explore the Paldea region freely without a linear path, tackling objectives in any order.
Terastal Phenomenon: A new mechanic that allows Pokémon to "terastallize," changing their appearance and boosting their moves based on their Tera Type, enhancing strategic play.
Three Story Paths: Players can choose from three main storylines:
1. Victory Road: Traditional Gym challenge to become Champion.
2. Path of Legends: Questing for Herba Mystica while battling Titan Pokémon.
3. Starfall Street: Taking on Team Star, a group of rebellious students.
Pokemon Camps: Similar to Generation 8, players can set up camps to interact with their Pokémon and cook meals.
Co-Op Multiplayer: Up to four players can explore the region together, battling and catching Pokémon.

Legendary Pokemon:

Main Legendaries: Koraidon (Scarlet) and Miraidon (Violet).

Other Notable Pokemon: New Paradox Pokémon, which are ancient or futuristic versions of existing Pokémon.

Villain:

Team Star: A group of rebellious students causing trouble, with various leaders to confront.

Legacy:

Generation 9 is significant for its open-world gameplay and the introduction of the Terastal mechanic, providing a new layer of strategy. The Paldea region is inspired by the Iberian Peninsula, featuring diverse landscapes and cultures. The flexibility in story progression and the multiplayer aspect have received positive feedback, though the games faced criticism regarding technical performance. Overall, it represents a new direction for the series, emphasizing exploration and player choice.
