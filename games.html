Spin-off Games

These games explore different genres and gameplays styles within the Pokemon Universe.

Pokémon Mystery Dungeon (various titles): A series of dungeon-crawling RPGs where players take on the role of a Pokémon.
Pokémon GO (2016): An augmented reality game for mobile devices that encourages players to catch Pokémon in the real world.
Pokémon Snap (1999, 2021): A photography game where players capture images of Pokémon in their natural habitats.
Pokémon Unite (2021): A multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game where teams compete to score points by defeating wild Pokémon and opposing players.
Pokémon Cafe ReMix (2020): A puzzle game where players run a café with Pokémon, solving puzzles to serve dishes.
Pokémon Masters EX (2019): A mobile game featuring trainers from various generations in battles.

Pokemon Trading Card Game

This collectible card game allows players to build decks and battle against each other using Pokémon cards. There are also video game adaptations, including Pokémon Trading Card Game Online and Pokémon Trading Card Game Live.

Pokemon Stadium and Batlle Revolution

Pokémon Stadium (N64, 1998): A 3D battling game featuring Pokémon from the original games.
Pokémon Battle Revolution (Wii, 2007): A battle-focused game that allows players to use their Pokémon from the main series games.

Pokemon Adventures and Spin-off

Pokémon Conquest (2012): A tactical RPG that combines Pokémon with the historical setting of the Warlord era in Japan.
Pokémon Quest (2018): An action-adventure game with a blocky art style, focusing on exploration and collecting Pokémon.
